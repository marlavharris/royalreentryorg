Direct Reentry Support

Royal Chances Reentry Foundation provides client-centered support intended to reduce recidivism and increase stability for justice-involved individuals across the Commonwealth.

We understand the real barriers to reentry. Our team provides structured resource navigation, weekly check-ins, appointment reminders, and specialized coaching to help individuals secure employment, housing, and long-term stability.

"Rooted in lived experience, our approach prioritizes dignity, clarity, and empowerment—helping participants focus on their future, not just their past."

Felony Explanation Letter Training

Royal Chances Reentry Foundation specializes in **Felony Explanation Letter Training**, a structured service designed to support justice-impacted individuals in professionally explaining their criminal history to employers, housing providers, licensing boards, and educational institutions.

This training guides participants through the process of clearly communicating their past without self-sabotage, over-disclosure, or shame.

Training Focus Areas:

  • Using clear, professional, and employer-appropriate language
  • Explaining convictions in a factual, non-defensive manner
  • Accountability without over-explaining details
  • Highlighting rehabilitation and positive life changes
  • Aligning disclosure with fair-chance hiring practices

Each participant receives individualized support to develop a customized letter adapted for their specific needs.
