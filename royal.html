Royal Chances Reentry Foundation provides the education, mentorship, and workforce development needed to restore dignity and ensure long-term stability.

Our Services Our Mission

Urban Learning & Resource Center

We operate on the ground, delivering life skills and career pathways directly to the returning citizen population.

Royal Chances Reentry Foundation is more than a nonprofit—it is a community hub led by those with lived experience. We understand the hurdles of reintegration because we have cleared them ourselves.

"To empower all returning citizens through lived-experience–led education, job readiness, and personal development designed to restore dignity and reduce recidivism."

Our clinical staff, outreach workers, and volunteers work tirelessly to provide job placement, public speaking training, and counseling referrals that actually move the needle.
