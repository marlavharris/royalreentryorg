Our Story & Mission

Royal Chances Reentry Foundation operates as a Learning and Resource Center providing workforce development, education, and life skills support for returning citizens.

The organization employs outreach workers, clinical staff, and volunteers to deliver programs including job placement, resume development, interview preparation, public speaking, and counseling referrals.

Expert Training

Specialized Felony Explanation Letter Training

One of the most significant barriers to employment for returning citizens is effectively communicating their past while highlighting their transformation.

Ownership: Learn to speak about your history with honesty and integrity.

Transformation: Highlight the specific steps and growth achieved since your conviction.

Value Proposition: Connect your lived experience to the resilience and work ethic employers value.
